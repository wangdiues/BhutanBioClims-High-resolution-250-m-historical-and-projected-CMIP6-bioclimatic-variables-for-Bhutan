# Hugging Face Dataset Card (Draft)

## Dataset summary
Bhutan CMIP6 BIOCLIM infrastructure outputs at 250 m, including per-GCM BIOCLIM rasters, multi-model ensembles, QC artifacts, and variable screening outputs.

## Source data
- Primary Source Citation (APA 7): Dorji, S., Stewart, S., Bajwa, A., Aziz, A., Shabbir, A., & Adkins, S. (2025). High-resolution (250 m) historical and projected (CMIP6) air temperature and precipitation grids for Bhutan (v1) [Data set]. CSIRO. https://doi.org/10.25919/pec2-hs50
- Source DOI: https://doi.org/10.25919/pec2-hs50

## Data splits (logical)
- Historical baseline: 1986_2015/historical
- Future projections: 2021_2050, 2051_2080, 2071_2100 with ssp126/ssp245/ssp370/ssp585
